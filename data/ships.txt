# Frontier Forge Ships
#
# A decentralized network of independent shipwrights, garage builders,
# and frontier machinists who build custom ships outside corporate
# control. The Firefly line covers honest trade — small transports
# and freighters with nacelle silhouettes and reinforced hulls. The
# Draugr and Marauder are what happens when free people build for war.
# 
#

#   "Forge Draugr" (Medium Warship)     — aggressive raider, 4G+3T+1Bay
#   "Forge Marauder" (Heavy Warship)    — troop carrier + 2 fighter bays
#   "Forge Crusader" (Heavy Warship)   — turret fortress, 12T
